Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last updated: July 21, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Fily Pro is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our file conversion services:

  • File Data: We temporarily process your uploaded files solely for conversion purposes
  • Usage Statistics: Basic anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, and access times for security and performance

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Converting your files to the requested format
  • Providing download links for converted files
  • Improving our service quality and performance
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse

3. File Storage and Retention

We maintain strict data retention policies:

  • Temporary Storage: Uploaded files are stored temporarily only during the conversion process
  • Automatic Deletion: All files are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours
  • No Long-term Storage: We do not permanently store your files or personal documents
  • Secure Processing: All file processing occurs in a secure, isolated environment

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/SSL
  • Secure file processing in isolated environments
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring systems

5. Third-Party Services

Fily Pro operates independently and does not share your files with third-party services. We may use:

  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) for faster loading
  • Analytics services for anonymous usage statistics
  • Security services for protection against threats

None of these services have access to your uploaded files.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data processing
  • Request deletion of your data (though files are auto-deleted)
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Session management during file conversion
  • Basic analytics (anonymous)
  • Security and fraud prevention

No personal information is stored in cookies.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@filypro.com
  • Telegram: @DeepInkTeam
  • GitHub: DeepInk Team

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying a notice on our website
  • Sending notifications for significant changes
